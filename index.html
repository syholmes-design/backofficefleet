The Problems Aren't Random. They're Predictable.

Most fleets do not lose money because they lack software. They lose money because execution in the field is inconsistent, proof is incomplete, and the back office is forced to chase exceptions after the fact.

  • Missing pre-trip verification
  • Wrong trailer or equipment mismatch
  • Seal or cargo documentation errors
  • Incomplete POD packets and delayed billing
  • Compliance exposure and roadside risk
  • Leadership time wasted on administrative firefighting
